Erwin Schrödinger

About Schrodinger

Erwin Rudolf Josef Alexander (Schrödinger 12 August 1887 - 4 January 1961), sometimes written as Erwin Schrodinger or Erwin Schroedinger, was a Nobel Prize-winning Austrian-Irish physicist who developed a number of fundamental results in quantum theory.

The Schrödinger equation provides a way to calculate the wave function of a system and how it changes dynamically in time.
